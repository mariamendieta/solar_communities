About Maria Mendieta

Maria Mendieta is a data scientist with an economics and energy policy background. She recently graduated from the data science immersive program at Galvanize. For her final project at Galvanize, Maria worked with an energy startup to cluster NY State communities based on their propensity to install solar panels. Before Galvanize, Maria was in Washington DC working for a multilateral bank, evaluating the impact of climate change and energy projects in Latin America. Maria is enthusiastic about using data science in the sustainable energy industry.
